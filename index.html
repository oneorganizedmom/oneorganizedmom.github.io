About Us

At One Organized Mom, your happiness is always our number one priority. We hold ourselves to high standards and take great pride in our work. We only hire professionals who want what we want: satisfied customers! Our crews are fully supervised and we create a "worry-free" environment for our clients. We take security very seriously. Each of our employees must pass a complete background screening:

  • Criminal Background Screen
  • Motor Vehicle Record Check
  • Personality Assessment
  • I-9 Compliance with no less than two acceptable forms of identification

We recruit and hire only the best of the best and uphold a stringent level of quality control. Our confidence in our team of associates and level of customer service is exemplary. It is because of this that we guarantee 100% satisfaction, no questions asked.

Our goal is to develop long-lasting relationships with our clients. We want you to be part of that experience and the level of personal care that is often neglected in today's busy world. We are passionate about our work and it shows!

Housekeeping Services

We offer a personal touch to ensure that your needs are taken care of every step of the way. Our Housekeeping Services include a 50+ point inspection every time we clean your home. These points include:

  • Vacuum and mop floors
  • Organize any clutter
  • Disinfect all handles, knobs and light switches
  • Dust lamps and lampshades, including light bulbs
  • Empty trash

KITCHEN

  • Clear and clean countertops
  • Clean exteriors of appliances and inside of microwaves
  • Wipe down tables, chairs, windowsills and inside of windows
  • Dust ceiling fans
  • Scrub range top and sink
  • Clean/load dishes into dishwasher or put away clean dishes

BATHROOMS

  • Clear and clean countertops
  • Clean mirrors and toilets
  • Scrub showers and bath tubs
  • Wipe down cabinets
  • Dust pictures, treasures and light fixtures

BEDROOMS

  • Dust ceiling fans, pictures, treasures, windowsills and ledges
  • Clear and dust tables, desks, media and beds
  • Clean mirrors
  • Fold clothes on flood/bed and place on chair/bed
  • Beds made, change sheets if fresh ones are put out by the client

LIVING AREAS

  • Dust ceiling fans, pictures, treasures and windowsills
  • Clear and dust tables, desks and media
  • Wipe down sofas and furniture

OTHER AREAS

  • Upholstered furniture vacuumed
  • Cushions and pillows fluffed
  • Glass tables cleaned and free of fingerprints
  • All accessories dusted and returned to the found locations
  • Dust window sills

Deep & Move-out Cleaning

Deep cleanings include all of the items offered in our Housekeeping Services in addition to:

  • Hand wipe baseboards
  • Hand wipe blinds
  • Vacuum under furniture

Please Note

  • Out staff doesn't climb higher than a two-step ladder which OOM supplies at every clean
  • We do our best to reach under raised pieces, but we do not move furniture to ensure the integrity of your home
  • If ovens and/or stovetops are cleaned, we cannot guarantee that they will be back to pristine condition due to the possible buildup and/or corrosion
  • Please remember we guarantee our services: if you are unhappy with your clean and call within 24 hours, we will be happy to come back and correct any issue
